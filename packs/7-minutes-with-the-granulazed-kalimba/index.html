7 minutes with the granulized Kalimba

What a day. After sitting around for hours watching the sparkling wine running through my hand and nearly eating a glass fragment by accident, I decided to fragment some sound. So I took a smooth Kalimba sample and loaded it into the Qu-Bit Nebulae module.

I modulated this nice piece of hardware using the OCP and sent the signal through the Dust Collector box… adding some spring reverb.. just for you.
