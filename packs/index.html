my little sounds & packs

Even my granny is creating sample packs and putting them on Gumroad – I want to be part of this sample pack movement.. So I went with the flow and placed some WAV files here. They come from jam sessions, field recording or my fork when it hit the ground during cooking.

7 minutes with the granulized Kalimba

Another 7 minutes of my life spent wisely with a single Kalimba sample. I ran it through the Nebulae, modulated it with the OCP and ran it through the Dust Collectors Spring Reverb

12 minutes with the Bastl Kastle

Sounds from the Bastl Instruments Kastl instrument. I soldered it during Superbooth and can now provide you with the finest sample pack I am capable of producing.
