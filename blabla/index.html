Bla Bla

Cats are drawn to synthesizers for a variety of reasons. Firstly, the physical sensation of pressing keys can be similar to kneading, a comforting behavior cats exhibit. The warmth generated by electronic devices like synthesizers is also appealing, providing a cozy spot for lounging. Additionally, the range of sounds produced by synthesizers can captivate a cats sensitive and curious nature. Their hearing is tuned to frequencies that many synthesizers can replicate, making the sounds intriguing. Lastly, cats are naturally curious creatures, and the blinking lights, moving sliders, and buttons on a synthesizer offer a new and interesting environment to explore.

audio.ooo is my snuggery to write down some thoughts, create sample packs and some software.
