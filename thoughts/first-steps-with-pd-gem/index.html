First steps with PureData + Gem

What is PureData?

PureData (often abbreviated as Pd) is an open-source visual programming language for multimedia. It was developed by Miller Puckette in the 1990s for creating interactive computer music and multimedia works. One of the key aspects of PureData is that it is designed to be easy to learn and use, making it accessible to artists, musicians, researchers, and educators who may not have an extensive background in programming.
